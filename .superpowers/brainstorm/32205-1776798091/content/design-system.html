Texture & Atmosphere
WOOD GRAIN TEXTURE
Subtle diagonal pattern on hero and section backgrounds. Feels like a barn wood dance floor.
VIGNETTE / SPOTLIGHT
Radial glow on hero centered on the logo — like a stage spotlight. Draws the eye in.
DISTRESSED DIVIDERS
Gold horizontal rules with rough edges between sections — like old western signage.
WARM INNER GLOW
Cards and the booking form have a subtle amber inner shadow — feels warm and intimate like candlelight.
